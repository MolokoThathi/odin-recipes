Chakalaka

Description

A South African stew that can be eaten with almost any food. It's mainly served with pap and some sort of meat which is normally boerewors (A sausage-like meat which is cooked when having a barbacue).

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil, or as needed
  • 3 diced onions
  • 3 diced green peppers
  • 3 diced carrots
  • 2 chopped green chilles
  • ¼ cup of water
  • 1 cube vegetable bouillon
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Stir in onions and bell peppers to the skillet. stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent
  3. Add the following at the same time:
    • carrots
    • tomatoes
    • green chillis
    • water
    • bouillon cube
    • curry powder
    • chili powder
    • salt and pepper
  4. Cook and stir for 15 to 20 minutes
