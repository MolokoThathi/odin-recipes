Pasta

Description

This recipe provide a cook with all the ingredients needed to create their own pasta from scratch without needing to purchased pasta, thus giving the cook all the flexibility to add whatever ingredients they love to the mixture. Well-made pasta from scratch.

Ingredients

  • Pasta cutter machine
  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 tablespoons olive Oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons water

Steps

  1. Mix flour, eggs, olive oul and salt in a bowl until combined.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of water at a time to the mixture until smooth, thick dough forms
  3. Turn dough onto a lightly floured work surface and knead for 10 minutes
  4. Divide dough into 8 balls
  5. Use the pasta machine to cut into desired pieces
